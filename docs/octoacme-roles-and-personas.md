# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers craft intuitive, user-centered experiences by conducting research, creating wireframes and prototypes, and ensuring design consistency across the product. They bridge user needs and technical implementation.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Design wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Collaborate with Product Managers to validate user flows
- Work with Developers to ensure design feasibility and consistency
- Maintain design systems and accessibility standards

### Goals
- Improve user satisfaction and reduce friction
- Ensure accessible, inclusive design
- Maintain consistent visual and interaction patterns

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product and Engineering teams
- User research readouts and persona documentation
- Collaboration via design tools (Figma, Sketch) with annotations
- Participation in sprint planning and retrospectives

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers create clear, accurate documentation for developers, end users, and internal teams. They ensure that features are well-documented, discoverable, and maintainable.

### Responsibilities
- Write and maintain user guides, API documentation, and tutorials
- Collaborate with Developers and Product Managers to understand features
- Review PRs for documentation accuracy and completeness
- Organize and structure knowledge bases and docs sites
- Gather feedback and iterate on documentation quality

### Goals
- Reduce time to onboard and resolve user questions
- Maintain up-to-date, accurate documentation
- Improve discoverability and usability of docs

### Typical Communication
- Documentation reviews in PRs and issue comments
- Kickoff and planning meetings to understand scope
- Regular syncs with Product and Engineering for updates
- Feedback loops with Support teams

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain the infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and automation that enable fast, reliable delivery. They ensure systems are observable, scalable, and secure.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage cloud infrastructure and deployment automation
- Implement monitoring, alerting, and logging
- Ensure security, compliance, and cost optimization
- Support incident response and root cause analysis

### Goals
- Minimize deployment time and risk
- Maximize system reliability and uptime
- Enable self-service and automation for developers

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- Incident postmortems and runbook documentation
- Infrastructure reviews and capacity planning sessions
- Collaboration via infrastructure-as-code PRs

---

## Support Lead

### Role Summary
Support Leads manage customer-facing support operations, triage issues, and serve as a critical feedback channel from users back to Product and Engineering teams.

### Responsibilities
- Triage and prioritize incoming support requests
- Coordinate with Engineering to resolve bugs and escalations
- Document known issues and workarounds
- Gather and communicate user feedback and pain points
- Train support team members on new features

### Goals
- Reduce time to resolution and improve customer satisfaction
- Identify patterns in user issues to inform product improvements
- Maintain clear escalation paths and SLAs

### Typical Communication
- Weekly syncs with Product and Engineering leads
- Bug reports and feature requests via issues
- Release briefings and training sessions
- Real-time collaboration in incident channels

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts turn data into actionable insights by building dashboards, running experiments, and measuring product success. They enable evidence-based decision-making across the organization.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track key product and business metrics
- Build dashboards and reports for stakeholders
- Conduct A/B tests and experiments
- Collaborate with Product Managers on success criteria
- Identify trends, anomalies, and opportunities

### Goals
- Enable data-driven prioritization and decisions
- Measure impact of features and validate hypotheses
- Improve visibility into user behavior and business health

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metric reviews with Product and leadership
- Experiment design and results presentations
- Ad-hoc analysis requests and data validation
- Documentation of metrics definitions and methodologies
